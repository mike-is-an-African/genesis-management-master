## Challenge

The National Employment Council (NEC) faced significant operational challenges with their outdated supplier management system:

- Entirely paper-based supplier database requiring manual management
- Inefficient supplier search and retrieval processes
- Limited data analysis capabilities for strategic decision-making
- Risk of data loss, duplication, and inconsistencies
- Difficulty in maintaining compliance with procurement regulations
