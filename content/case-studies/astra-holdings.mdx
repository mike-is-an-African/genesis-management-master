## Challenge

Astra Holdings Limited, a ZSE-listed group comprising four diverse operating units (Astra Paints, Astra Chemical, Chemical Enterprises & NCP Distillers), faced significant financial management challenges:

- Complex financial reporting requirements as a publicly listed company
- Diverse accounting needs across multiple business units
- Intricate tax obligations across various manufacturing segments
- Need for standardized bookkeeping across all operating units
- Compliance with Zimbabwe's evolving corporate tax regulations
