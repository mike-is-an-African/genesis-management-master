## Challenge

Lilly Harvest, an agricultural and food company with operations spanning Zimbabwe, South Africa, and Mozambique, faced significant financial management challenges:

- Complex tax compliance requirements across multiple jurisdictions
- Inconsistent accounting practices between country operations
- Currency fluctuations affecting financial reporting and planning
- Limited visibility into cross-border financial performance
- Difficulty in optimizing tax positions while maintaining compliance
