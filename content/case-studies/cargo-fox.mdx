## Challenge

Cargo Fox, a retail company operating in Zimbabwe and South Africa, faced complex operational challenges in managing their cross-border business:

- Intricate customs requirements and frequently changing import/export regulations
- Inefficient documentation processes leading to border delays and additional costs
- Complex tax implications of cross-border transactions
- Difficulties in optimizing duty payments while maintaining full compliance
- Need for unified accounting and financial reporting across operations in two countries
